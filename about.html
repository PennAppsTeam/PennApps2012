What is PennApps?

PennApps is the University of Pennsylvania's bi-annual hackathon event. The University's best programmers and entrepreneurs will team up over a weekend to build amazing applications. The event is hosted by the Dining Philosophers (Penn's computer science club) and other campus groups.

A brief history.

This upcoming Hackathon will be the third PennApps Hackathon. In the past (2009, 2010, Spring 2011) PennApps has seen a tremendous response from the students and the tech community. PennApps Mobile, held in January 2011, had nearly 100 attendees and 23 teams presenting demos at the end. Technically Philly, amongst others, has covered the event. We couldn't have done it without our fantastic sponsors at our last two hackathons!

ScratchTable

Grand Prize ($2500)

Turn any surface into a touch input surface. Utilizing only a microphone, we can transform objects like walls and tables into a touchpad. It's like a touchscreen but without the screen! ScratchTable is a demo of this technology in the form of a virtual turntable.

Grassroutes

Second Place ($1500)

We know that the most effective way to make anything happen is by reaching out to your elected officials, and we also know that this can be a lot of work. A lot of well-minded, like-minded people will visit your website, but few will take action. Grassroutes helps convert those good intentions into action.

rdtripp.in

Third Place ($1000)

Planning a road trip? rdtripp.in will help you plot a route to see your friends along the way.
